Homemade Garlic Bread

Savory Buttery Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • Loaf of Bread of Your Choosing/li>
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • Cheese (No limit...)

Directions

  1. Cut loaf of bread to desired sizes
  2. Add oil and butter to the bread slices
  3. Top with as much cheese as your heart desires...
  4. Bake in the oven on 350 for 7 minutes
