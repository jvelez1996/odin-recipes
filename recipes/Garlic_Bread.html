Homemade Garlic Bread

Savory Buttery Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 12 oz can of Tomato Paste
  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Store Bought Crust
  • Cheese (No limit...)

Directions

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Lather the crust with your sauce
  3. Top with as much cheese as your heart desires...
  4. Bake in the oven on 350 for 18 minutes
