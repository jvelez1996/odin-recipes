Really Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo

Incrediby creamy and delicious alfredo. Created with a homemade sauce and grilled chicken, this alfredo is guranteed to be a hit with your family!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Step 2 In a medium saucepan, combine cream cheese, Parmesan, margarine, milk, garlic powder and basil and stir over low heat until smooth and creamy, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Toss sauce with pasta and sprinkle with nutmeg.
  4. Serve.
